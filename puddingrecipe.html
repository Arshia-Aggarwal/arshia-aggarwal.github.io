FRUIT CARAMEL PUDDING

INGREDIENTS:

Vanila ﬂavor sponge cake
Any ﬂavor juice
Whipped cream
Fruit jam
Any type of chopped fruits
Almond caramel crunch

METHOD:

We have to cut the cake into two pieces horizontally. Set one vanilla cake piece in a bowl. Then wet it with juice. Apply whipped cream on it.Add chopped fruits on it. Then again place a cake layer. Add whipped cream. Use some fruit jam to enrich the cake. At last garnish pudding's half part with fruits and the other part with caramel almonds crunch. Your delicious pudding is ready to serve.
